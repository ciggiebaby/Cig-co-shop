About

Cig & Co. transforms the iconic paisley motif into collectible leather goods, jewelry, and refined accessories. Rooted in timeless design rather than fleeting trends, every piece is created as a work of functional art—crafted to be worn, displayed, passed down, and remembered.

Produced in limited quantities and finished with uncompromising attention to detail, each release is intentionally scarce. When a collection sells through, it becomes part of the archive. No promises of restocks. Only the opportunity to own a piece of the story.
